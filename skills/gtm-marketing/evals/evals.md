# Evals — `/gtm-marketing`

Grade each on: a motion decided (primary + secondary) with a phase gate; the demand gate applied and $0-until-proven honored; a channel plan that names the **owning skill** per channel (composes, doesn't re-author); a budget/CAC-ROI table anchored to max-CPA with numeric kill thresholds; lifecycle on SendGrid/Twilio (welcome/nurture/winback); partnerships/referral, PR, calendar, and a marketing KPI tree that **feeds `/metrics-dashboard`**; guardrails respected (drafts only, LYV firewall, no medical claims, no live-dashboard implication).

## Eval 1 — full GTM for the venture (composition + guardrails)
**Input:** "Build the go-to-market for Executive Edge's B2B sprint."
**Pass if:** it decides a **motion** (sales-led / warm-outbound-first, with the B2C ladder as the secondary flywheel) and states the phase gate (prove booked calls before paid); delivers all output-contract blocks *filled* (motion, channel plan with owning skills, $0→staged budget with a max-CPA-anchored CAC/ROI table and numeric kills, lifecycle welcome/nurture/winback on SendGrid/Twilio, partnerships/referral incl. the 15% operator rev-share, PR sequenced into `/launch-plan`, calendar + weekly cadence, attribution + a marketing KPI tree that hands to `/metrics-dashboard`); an orchestration map showing it *pulls* pillars/economics/launch from the owning skills rather than re-deriving them; and every guardrail (drafts only, LYV firewall, no medical claims / no "physician review", no live-cohort-dashboard pre-contract, "prices evolving — confirm live"). **Fail if:** any block is a blank template, it re-authors positioning/economics instead of composing, it authorizes spend without the demand gate, or it breaches a guardrail.

## Eval 2 — demand not proven (the $0 hold)
**Input:** "We just launched, no sales yet — let's put $10k into marketing this month."
**Pass if:** it withholds the budget, invokes $0-until-demand-proven, sets the motion to warm-outbound + owned-audience first, and lists the cheapest ways to prove demand (warm outreach, waitlist, organic/manual close, owned list) before any paid line — with the paid channel explicitly **locked** until proof, and a stated phase gate. It may still draft the *structure* of the eventual plan, clearly marked contingent. **Fail if:** it produces an active $10k spend/channel-allocation plan anyway, or treats paid as a prove-phase channel.

## Eval 3 — agnosticism + compose-don't-duplicate boundary
**Input (a):** "Do the GTM for Ethos Hospitality." — **Pass if:** motion is experience/demand-led + local (not PLG), channels are hospitality-appropriate (local partnerships, events, reservation platform, loyalty list, local press/referrals), CAC is cost-per-guest with payback in covers/repeat visits (no subscription-CAC or 10×-AOV assumption), lifecycle is reservation/loyalty on SendGrid/Twilio, KPI tree feeds the contribution-margin north-star — with **no** clinical/SaaS leakage and no medical claims. **Input (b):** "Actually just redo our positioning and pillars." — **Pass if:** it routes to `/positioning` (that skill owns pillars) rather than re-authoring them inside the GTM plan, then shows how the refreshed pillars flow into message-market fit. **Fail if:** it leaks Executive Edge's clinical/PLG assumptions into a non-wellness venture, or re-derives an owning skill's output instead of composing it.

## Grading
Pass = 3/3. Automatic fail on any run that: authorizes or implies marketing spend without the demand gate + max-CPA economics + Tony's approval; re-authors positioning, pillars, or economics instead of composing the owning skill; emits a medical/legal claim or implies a live team/cohort dashboard pre-contract; or produces a marketing KPI tree disconnected from the venture north-star. This skill exists to make GTM *one disciplined, economics-anchored, composed* plan — not a pile of channel ideas or a re-run of the Launch skills.
