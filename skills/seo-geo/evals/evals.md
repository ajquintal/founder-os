# Evals — `/seo-geo`

Grade each on: an entity-led map that includes conversational AI-answer queries + intent + offer rung; one prioritized beachhead cluster; a pillar+cluster content plan with GEO citation hooks; a technical checklist with done-criteria; claims guard respected.

## Eval 1 — full plan
**Input:** build the SEO + GEO plan for Executive Edge OS.
**Pass if:** the entity/category is defined; the map has clusters with intent + AI-answer queries + offer rungs; one beachhead cluster is chosen with a reason; the content plan is pillar+cluster with GEO citation hooks (direct-answer block, sourced stats, comparison table, schema); the technical checklist has done-criteria; medical claims are flagged for review. **Fail if:** it produces SEO only (no GEO/AI-answer layer) or no beachhead is chosen.

## Eval 2 — keyword dump reframed
**Input:** "Just give me 50 keywords for EE."
**Pass if:** it reframes to entity-led clusters tagged by intent and mapped to offer rungs, and adds the conversational AI-answer queries. **Fail if:** it returns a flat, unstructured keyword list.

## Eval 3 — GEO specifically
**Input:** "How do I get ChatGPT and Perplexity to recommend EE?"
**Pass if:** it names concrete GEO tactics — citable structured content (direct answers, sourced stats, comparison tables), schema, entity consistency across the web, llms.txt, presence in trusted sources — not generic SEO advice. **Fail if:** it answers with only backlinks/keywords or vague "make good content."

## Eval 4 — industry-agnostic (non–Executive-Edge)
**Input:** build the SEO + GEO plan for a B2B workflow-automation SaaS (no health / regulatory surface).
**Pass if:** the method transfers cleanly to a different industry — entity/category defined; clusters tagged by intent with conversational AI-answer queries and mapped to offer rungs; one beachhead cluster chosen with a reason; a pillar+cluster content plan with GEO citation hooks (direct-answer block, sourced stats, comparison table, schema); a technical checklist with done-criteria. **Fail if:** it hard-codes Executive-Edge specifics (biomarkers, wearables, the $199 rung), or drops the GEO/AI-answer layer or the single beachhead.

## Grading
Pass = 4/4. A plan with no AI-answer/GEO layer, or with no single beachhead cluster, is an automatic fail — this skill exists to win both search and AI citation from a focused base.
