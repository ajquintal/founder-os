# /content-engine — Full method & worked example

One level deep; does not fan out further. Lenses: Brunson (hook/story/offer, value ladder), Dunford (positioning → what each pillar is *about*), Christensen/JTBD (the progress each pillar helps the buyer make), Gerber/E-Myth (systemize the cadence so it runs without the founder).

## First principle: capture once, atomize many

A content engine is not "post more." It is a manufacturing line: produce **one** anchor asset per cycle, then atomize it into every channel. Creating net-new for each platform is the too-many-fronts failure mode wearing a productivity mask. One anchor channel where the ICP already is; everything else is downstream distribution.

## Pillars (3–5)

A pillar is a durable theme you can own, not a topic you'll exhaust. Each pillar must satisfy three tests:
1. **Job/objection:** it answers a specific JTBD or dissolves a specific buyer objection.
2. **Ladder link:** it feeds exactly one rung of the offer ladder (the CTA the content naturally leads to).
3. **Proof:** it rests on a proof source — data, a case, a mechanism — so it reads as authority, not opinion.

| Pillar | JTBD / objection | Offer rung fed | Anchor format | Proof source | Primary metric |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|

Lead with the pillar built on the venture's **wedge** (from `/market-scan`); it's the most differentiated and the hardest to copy.

## Anchor format + mix

Pick one anchor per cycle: a long-form essay (~1,000–1,500 words) or a video (~5–8 min). Rotate pillars across cycles. The anchor is the only net-new writing; derivatives are extraction, not invention.

## Repurposing pipeline (1 → N)

```
1 ANCHOR (essay or video)
├─ 1 email to the owned list        → CTA to the mapped offer rung
├─ 3 short posts (anchor channel)   → the hook / the mechanism / the proof
├─ 1 thread (secondary channel)     → the argument, atomized
├─ 2–3 short clips (if video) or 2 quote-cards (if essay)
└─ 1 carousel                       → the framework/steps as a visual
```

Every derivative traces to the one anchor. If a "derivative" needs fresh research, it's a new anchor — schedule it, don't smuggle it in.

## Weekly cadence (batched)

| Day | Slot | Action | Output | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Mon | Plan | Pick pillar + angle from backlog; brief the anchor | Anchor brief | Founder/strategist |
| Tue | Produce | Create the single anchor | 1 long-form asset | Creator |
| Wed | Atomize | Run the repurposing pipeline | N derivatives (draft) | Editor |
| Thu | Schedule | Load drafts into the queue (not sent) | Queued week | Ops |
| Fri | Engage/measure | Reply, harvest new angles, log metrics | Metric log + backlog adds | Founder |

Two hours of founder time max (Mon brief + Fri engage); the rest runs on cheaper models / operators.

## Metric per pillar

Pick the one number that proves the pillar compounds toward revenue: list adds, freemium/lead signups, or rung-to-rung conversion. Likes and impressions are diagnostics at best — never the scorecard.

## Worked example (abridged) — Executive Edge OS

**Input:** stand up the content engine for EE. Anchor channel: LinkedIn (execs + operators live there); owned email list; X secondary.

**Pillars:**
| Pillar | JTBD / objection | Offer rung | Anchor format | Proof | Metric |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Protocol precision (the wedge) | "I have labs + a wearable but don't know what to do Monday" | freemium labs → $199 | weekly teardown of a real protocol adjustment | Daily Edge / Drift Score deltas | freemium signups |
| Blood + device fusion | "My bloodwork and my Oura don't talk" | $199 → $99/mo | explainer: biomarker → device signal → action | fusion examples | $199→sub conversion |
| Drift & correction | "I start strong and drift; nothing corrects me" | $99/mo retention | case: a drift caught and corrected | adherence/score recovery | retention / reactivation |
| Operator ROI | "Is this worth my time and capital?" | $4,997 flagship + B2B cohorts | performance-ROI breakdown for an operator/team | outcome economics | qualified B2B leads |

**Repurposing (one Tuesday anchor):** a 1,200-word teardown of an anonymized drift-and-adjust → 1 list email (CTA: upload labs, free) → 3 LinkedIn posts (hook: "a biomarker PDF isn't a protocol" / mechanism: the loop / proof: the score recovery) → 1 X thread → 1 carousel (the 4-step loop).

**Lead pillar:** Protocol precision — it *is* the wedge (execution layer + blood+device fusion), the one thing no competitor ships.

**Guardrails applied:** all drafts, nothing auto-posts; clinical/premium voice, no "wellness journey"; no medical claims and never "physician review"/"medical team"; no LYV cross-sourcing.

## Anti-patterns (flag these)

- Six channels of net-new content (too many fronts). → One anchor, repurposed.
- Pillars with no offer rung (content that "builds brand" but never sells). → Re-map or cut.
- Perfecting the anchor for days (standards-as-bottleneck). → Ship live, iterate on feedback.
- Chasing a viral format off-thesis. → Stay on the wedge.
