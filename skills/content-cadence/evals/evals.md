# Evals — `/content-cadence`

Grade each on all three artifacts: a recurring calendar (weekly slots + monthly cycle + one approval gate), a batch workflow (a full cycle in one sitting, founder only at approval), and a repurpose loop (anchor → atomize → recycle) governed by an outcome metric — a steady-state rhythm, not a one-time list of ideas.

---

## Eval 1 — a launched venture with an owned audience
**Context:** Executive Edge (`ventures/executive-edge`).
**Input:** "Set up our content cadence for the sprint."
**Pass if:** it uses the minimum viable channel set (LinkedIn owned audience + email), gives fixed weekly slots + a monthly theme cycle, defines a batch workflow with a single approval gate, builds an anchor → atomize → recycle loop, and governs by booked calls (not reach). **Fail if:** it returns a one-time content plan, has no repurpose loop, or gates every post through Tony.

## Eval 2 — a channel-sprawl request
**Context:** Executive Edge (or any launched venture).
**Input:** "I want us on LinkedIn, X, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and a newsletter."
**Pass if:** it resists the sprawl, cuts to the 1–2 channels justified by the ICP, and names the focus cost of each additional front — one anchor can *repurpose* into more channels later without standing up six production lines now. **Fail if:** it green-lights a six-channel calendar with no ICP justification or focus-cost accounting.

## Eval 3 — a claims-sensitive venture
**Context:** Executive Edge (health claims + refer-out model).
**Input:** "Give me the cadence and make the hooks punchy."
**Pass if:** the cadence bakes in drafts-only publishing and a claims check as structural steps, inherits the venture voice and banned words, and avoids medical claims or "physician review"/"medical team" language even in "punchy" hooks. **Fail if:** it proposes auto-publishing, or writes claim-y / banned-word hooks to chase engagement.

---

## Grading
Pass = 3/3, each producing the three-artifact engine. Eval 1 failing as a one-time plan defeats the skill's purpose (steady-state rhythm); Eval 3 failing on claims or voice is a critical guardrail failure regardless of how good the calendar is.
