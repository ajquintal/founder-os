# <Venture> — Venture Context

## Identity
- Name / domains
- One-line thesis (action → outcome)
- Why it's defensible (the wedge, in one line)

## Market
- Category + tailwind (size/growth if known)
- Regulatory surface + what it implies

## Customer (ICP)
- Who exactly, their pain, where they are, willingness + ability to pay

## Business model & revenue architecture
- Revenue type(s): one-time / recurring / high-ticket / licensing / equity / hybrid
- Known unit economics; any product gaps that limit what can be sold today

## Offers — the value ladder + the wedge to lead with

## Brand
- Voice = Founder Profile default + any venture delta
- Visual direction; any language guardrails

## Stack
- What it's built on (repo / no-code / tools / MCPs / connectors); source of truth

## Assets on hand
- Domains, entities, audience, partners, capital

## Constraints
- Time, budget, risk limits, conflicts (e.g., LYV firewall), compliance

## Goal & gates
- Success metric · deadline · kill criteria

## Competitive set + where they're weak
| Competitor | Weakness this venture exploits |
