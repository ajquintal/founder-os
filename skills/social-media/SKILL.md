# /social-media — Platform-Specific Social Plan

The social execution layer. Pick the 1–2 platforms the ICP actually *buys* on, give each a strategy + format + frequency, map the pillars onto them, and hand Tony a paste-ready template pack + a 2-week starter calendar he only has to approve. This fills a founder blind spot: Tony is weak on social, so the system carries it — templates, not homework.

Sits **beneath** the content skills and does not duplicate them. `/content-engine` sets the pillars, the anchor channel, and the abstract 1→N pipeline (the one-time build); `/content-cadence` runs the steady-state rhythm. This skill supplies the platform-specific HOW — the per-platform playbooks, the native templates and hooks, the platform-native derivatives, the engagement routine, and the social KPI set — that neither of those specifies. Reads `/content-engine` (pillars + anchor + pipeline) and `/positioning` (the wedge every post repeats); feeds `/metrics-dashboard` (the social KPI set).

If you find yourself re-deriving pillars or a recurring calendar, stop — that belongs to the sibling.

## Method (full detail + worked example in `references/method.md`)
1. **Select platforms by ICP — pick 1–2, never spray.** Score each platform (LinkedIn, X, Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, …) on the fit matrix: where the ICP's *buying* attention sits, the native form the venture can actually produce on its constraints, and the venture's warmest existing asset. Choose ONE **anchor** + at most ONE **amplifier**; put the rest on an explicit **skip** list (too-many-fronts guard). **B2B default anchor = LinkedIn** — it is Tony's warmest owned audience (~3,217 connections; his personal-transformation post did strong numbers there).
2. **Write the per-platform playbook.** Per chosen platform: role (anchor/amplifier), objective, ranked native formats, sustainable frequency, what the platform rewards, and the one thing that matters most there.
3. **Map pillars → platforms.** Pull the 3–5 pillars from `/content-engine`; assign each to the platform(s) that carry it and the native format it takes there. Not every pillar goes everywhere; the wedge leads on the anchor.
4. **Build the template pack.** Per platform: a hook library + paste-ready post templates (LinkedIn POV/transformation, teardown, proof; X thread + standalone; IG Reel/carousel; short-form video) with `[BRACKETS]` to fill. This is the deliverable that carries the founder.
5. **Ship the 2-week starter calendar.** 10 working days — each slot: platform · format · pillar · working hook · owner · status(draft). Bootstraps the rhythm `/content-cadence` then runs.
6. **Show the anchor → platform-native fan-out.** Take ONE anchor asset and derive its platform-native pieces (thread, carousel, standalone posts, quote-cards, owned-list email) — the concrete realization of content-engine's 1→N.
7. **Set the engagement/community routine.** A time-boxed daily/weekly loop (reply to own comments → engage ICP/dream accounts → drafted DMs), voiced as the brand, drafts-only, ToS-safe; comments harvested into the content backlog.
8. **Define growth loops + the social KPI set.** Loops that compound to the OWNED audience (not followers); a leading-weighted KPI set with floor→action that feeds `/metrics-dashboard` — vanity counts (followers, impressions, likes) demoted to diagnostics.

## Output contract — the venture's social plan
1. **Platform selection** — the fit-matrix scores + the chosen anchor (+ optional amplifier) + the explicit skip list, each with its reason.
2. **Per-platform playbook** — role · objective · formats · frequency · what it rewards · the one thing that matters, per chosen platform.
3. **Pillar → platform map** — table: pillar · anchor-platform format · amplifier format · lead?
4. **Template pack** — hook libraries + paste-ready templates per platform, with `[BRACKETS]`. **[DRAFT — nothing publishes without approval]**
5. **2-week starter calendar** — table: day · platform · format · pillar · working hook · owner · status.
6. **Anchor → platform-native fan-out** — one anchor drawn out to its N platform-native derivatives.
7. **Engagement routine** — the daily/weekly time-boxed loop with owners.
8. **Growth loops + social KPIs** — the loops + a metrics block handed to `/metrics-dashboard` (leading indicators + a money strip), each KPI with source · cadence · floor→action.

## Rules
- **Drafts only — never auto-post.** Every post, thread, carousel, and DM is queued for approval; **one approval gate per batch, not per post** (protect Tony's ≤2 hrs/week). Publishing is his call.
- **Pick 1–2 platforms; the skip list is part of the deliverable.** A third platform is a new front — justify it against the ICP or cut it. Do not spray to "be everywhere."
- **Don't duplicate the content skills.** Pillars, anchor, and the abstract pipeline come from `/content-engine`; the recurring rhythm is `/content-cadence`. This skill adds only the platform-specific layer (platform selection, per-platform strategy, templates, hooks, starter calendar, native derivatives, engagement, social KPIs).
- **Voice per `founder-profile/voice-and-brand.md`** — clinical, precise, premium. Social's default is hype; hold the line: no banned words ("journey", "wellness", "hack", "game-changer", "revolutionary", "guru"), no emoji-spray, no engagement-bait ("comment 'YES'"), no fake urgency, no crying-testimonial emotionalism. Power vocabulary where it fits.
- **Platform-native, not cross-posted.** Each derivative is reformatted for its platform; the same text pasted on five platforms is not a plan.
- **Claims & capability (G-claims):** make only substantiated claims, and never imply a capability or deliverable the venture can't provide today. Honor the venture's own claims & regulatory constraints (from its `venture-context` + `engineering-backbone §9`); route any regulated claim (health / financial / legal / environmental) for professional review before publishing. Member-gated content ≠ public claims.
- **LYV firewall:** never source audience, engagement targets, or DM lists from LYV relationships for a solely-owned venture.
- **KPIs feed `/metrics-dashboard` and obey its rules:** leading-weighted, every metric carries a floor→action; followers/impressions/likes are diagnostics, never the scorecard — the loop closes on owned-audience adds and outcomes, not reach.
- **ToS-safe growth only.** No follow/unfollow bots, bought followers, or engagement-pod schemes (reputation guardrail).
- **Tooling:** visual derivatives route to **Canva MCP**; copy drafting can use **`marketing:content-creation`**; the owned-list email derivative is a **Gmail draft** (drafts-only). Prices from `/offer-architect`; if none, mark `[PRICE TBD]` and request it — never invent.
