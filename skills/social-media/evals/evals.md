# Evals — `/social-media`

Grade each on: 1–2 platforms chosen (anchor + ≤1 amplifier) with an explicit skip list and reasons; a per-platform playbook; pillars **pulled from `/content-engine`** (not invented) and mapped to platforms; a paste-ready template pack with hooks; a 2-week starter calendar with owners; platform-native derivatives (not cross-posts); a social KPI set that feeds `/metrics-dashboard` (leading-weighted, floor→action, no vanity scorecard); correct voice and guardrails (drafts-only, no medical/legal claims, LYV firewall).

## Eval 1 — B2B, pick the anchor
**Input:** set up social for Executive Edge's 90-day B2B sprint.
**Pass if:** it selects **LinkedIn as the anchor** and cites Tony's warm audience / transformation post as the reason; names at most one amplifier (X) and an explicit skip list (IG/TikTok/YouTube) with reasons; gives a per-platform playbook; pulls EE's pillars from `/content-engine` and maps them (wedge leads); delivers a paste-ready template pack (incl. the POV/transformation format) + a 2-week starter calendar with owners + status(draft); hands `/metrics-dashboard` a leading-weighted KPI set with floor→action where the scorecard is booked-calls/list-adds, not followers; everything drafts-only; **no medical claims, never "physician review"/"medical team"; no live team/cohort dashboard implied.** **Fail if:** it sprays across 4+ platforms, scorecards follower/impression counts, invents pillars instead of pulling from `/content-engine`, or emits medical claims or banned words.

## Eval 2 — resist the spray
**Input:** "I want to post daily on LinkedIn, X, Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube."
**Pass if:** it consolidates to 1 anchor + ≤1 amplifier, names the **too-many-fronts** failure mode explicitly, puts the rest on a skip list, and shows any surface it keeps is fed by the anchor's platform-native fan-out (not net-new); frequency is sized to what a cheap model can draft and Tony can approve in one batch. **Fail if:** it produces five independent net-new platform plans, or accepts "daily everywhere" without challenging it.

## Eval 3 — industry-agnostic + claims/voice (non–Executive-Edge)
**Input:** build the social plan for Ethos Hospitality (premium multi-concept restaurants + hotel F&B).
**Pass if:** anchor = **Instagram** (visual/experiential, affluent-diner + local discovery) with an appropriate amplifier or none; LinkedIn is correctly deprioritized (kept only for a later B2B licensing angle); templates are IG-native (Reel/carousel/Stories), not LinkedIn text posts; voice tuned warmer per the venture delta but still **no banned words**; the KPI set fits a non-subscription model (covers/reservations/events/loyalty, not MRR); **no clinical/health-tech framing leaks in**; drafts-only. **Fail if:** it defaults to LinkedIn out of habit, imports EE's clinical framing or pillars, or reuses subscription/MRR metrics that don't fit hospitality.

## Grading
Pass = 3/3. Automatic fail on any output that (a) sprays across 3+ platforms with no skip rationale, (b) makes a follower/impression/like count the scorecard, or (c) emits a medical/legal claim or a banned word — this skill exists to concentrate a founder-weak function onto 1–2 platforms, carry it with paste-ready templates, and measure it by outcomes, not reach.
