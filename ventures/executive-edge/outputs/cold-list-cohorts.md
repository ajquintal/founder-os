## The offer these targets are for

A 50–500-person company buys a **6-month Executive Cohort** for **5–10 of its senior leaders** on the Executive Edge OS platform. Baseline labs + quarterly retests, personalized protocols, a **private per-exec dashboard**, an **anonymized quarterly team readout for the CEO**, and a quarterly review call. Price **$30–48k / 6 mo (floor $30k)**.

- **Positioning — the "Executive Performance OS," not a lab test or a perk.** The wedge is the closed loop: **assign protocol → score execution → detect drift → adjust**, fused across **blood + continuous device data**. It's the system that runs the fix daily.
- **Buyer:** the CEO/owner of the company, motivated by **leadership performance, retention of senior talent, and key-person risk** — not a benefits line item.
- **Honest scope (do not overstate in any reply):** cohorts are delivered as individual client seats + a **manual, anonymized quarterly team readout** — **never promise a live self-serve team dashboard** until a contract is signed. No medical claims; never "physician review" / "medical team."
