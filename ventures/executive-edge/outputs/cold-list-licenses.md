A telehealth practice, functional-medicine/longevity clinic, or coaching/performance firm runs an **executive-health program on the Executive Edge engine** — the closed loop: **assign protocol → score execution → detect drift → adjust**, fused across blood + continuous device data. - **Honest scope (do not overstate):** this is the **engine, "Powered by Executive's Edge"** — **not** a full white-label app, and **not** a live self-serve team dashboard (roster reporting is a delivered quarterly summary). The **licensee owns clinical responsibility and provider relationships**; Executive Edge supplies software, scoring, and protocol tooling — not medical services. No medical claims; no "physician review" / "medical team."
